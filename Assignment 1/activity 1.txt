## My Favorite Sandwich

1.take out bread, turkey, mayo, spinach, tomato and knife
2. open bread bag
3. take out 2 slices of bread
4. close bag.
5. open turkey bag
6. take out 5 slices of turkey
7. place 5 peices of turkey on 1 slice of bread
8. close turkey bag
9. open bag of spinach
10. grab small handful of spinach
11. place spinach on top of turkey
12. close bag of spinach
13. grab knife and tomato
14. cut 2 slices of tomato
15. place 2 slices of tomato on top of spinach
16.set tomato aside
17. open jar of mayo
18. use knife to scoop 1 tablspoon of mayo on the remaining slice of bread
19. close jar of mayo
20. place slice of bread on top of the other with the mayo facing the tomato,spinach and turkey
21. put knife in sink
22. put away bread,turkey, spinach,mayo and tomato
23. consume the sandwich
